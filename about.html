About Marang SHEQ Consulting

Building safer, healthier and more sustainable workplaces.

Marang SHEQ Consulting is dedicated to supporting organisations in developing strong Safety, Health, Environmental and Quality (SHEQ) foundations, grounded in both international best practice and South African realities.

We bring together practical experience, technical expertise and a people-centred approach to help organisations move beyond compliance towards meaningful, sustained performance improvement.

Who we are

Marang SHEQ Consulting was established to provide organisations with accessible, expert support in managing their Safety, Health, Environmental and Quality responsibilities. We understand that each organisation operates in a unique context, with its own risks, constraints and opportunities.

Our role is to translate standards, legislation and best practices into practical solutions that work in your environment. We focus on building systems and processes that are simple to understand, easy to apply and sustainable over the long term.

From small businesses to larger enterprises, we partner with clients across sectors to strengthen SHEQ performance, reduce risk and support a culture of care and accountability.

What sets us apart
  • Hands-on experience in real operating environments.
  • Solutions tailored to South African legal requirements and industry context.
  • Focus on people, culture and behaviour – not just paperwork.
  • Support from initial assessment through to implementation and improvement.

Our mission

To partner with organisations in developing and maintaining effective SHEQ systems and capabilities that protect people, the environment and business value, while contributing positively to communities.

Our vision

To be a trusted SHEQ partner recognised for practical, people-centred support that enables organisations to achieve sustainable, world-class performance.

Our guiding principles

These principles shape how we engage with our clients and the value we aim to deliver.

Professionalism

We work with diligence, confidentiality and respect, maintaining high professional standards throughout every engagement.

Integrity

Our advice is objective and ethical, grounded in what is right for the people and organisations we serve over the long term.

Collaboration

We work alongside your teams, building internal ownership and capability rather than dependency on external consultants.

Continuous learning

We stay current with evolving standards, legislation and good practices so that the support we provide remains relevant and effective.

People behind the work

Our consultants combine formal qualifications with practical experience in SHEQ roles. We understand the realities of implementing systems in busy operations and work with you to find solutions that fit your people, processes and resources.

Whether you are starting from the basics or refining an established system, we’d be glad to explore how we can support you.
