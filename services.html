Our Services

SHEQ support tailored to your organisation’s needs.

We offer a focused range of services to help you assess, design, implement and improve your Safety, Health, Environmental and Quality management practices.

Services can be combined into a project, delivered as standalone interventions or provided through ongoing advisory support – depending on what will work best for your organisation.

Core service areas

From understanding where you are now to putting sustainable systems in place, we support you across the full SHEQ journey.

01
Audits & assessments

Independent assessments to understand your current level of compliance, risk and system maturity.

  • Baseline and issue-based risk assessments.
  • Legal compliance audits and inspections.
  • System and documentation gap analysis.
  • Follow-up reports with practical recommendations.
02
Management system design & development

Design of integrated SHEQ management systems that reflect your operations and align with recognised standards.

  • System framework and structure design.
  • Policies, procedures and process flows.
  • Operational controls and permits.
  • Integration with existing business systems.
03
Implementation & change support

Hands-on support to roll out new or updated SHEQ systems and embed them into day-to-day operations.

  • Implementation planning and phasing.
  • On-site and remote coaching for key roles.
  • Support with change management and communication.
  • Practical tools to support daily use.
04
Training & awareness

Tailored training to build knowledge, skills and awareness at all levels of the organisation.

  • Induction and general SHEQ awareness sessions.
  • Role-specific training for supervisors and managers.
  • Workshops on risk assessment and incident reporting.
  • Refresher training and toolbox topics.
05
Legal compliance support

Practical guidance to help you understand and manage your SHEQ-related legal obligations.

  • Compilation and maintenance of legal registers.
  • Evaluation of compliance and action planning.
  • Updates on relevant regulatory changes.
  • Support in preparing for inspections and audits.
06
Ongoing advisory & improvement support

Flexible support arrangements to help you sustain and continuously improve your SHEQ performance.

  • Periodic reviews and advisory sessions.
  • Support with internal audits and management reviews.
  • Assistance with incident investigations and corrective actions.
  • Retainer-based support for ongoing queries.

How we work with you

Every organisation is different. We take time to understand your operations, risks and priorities before proposing an approach.

The outline below shows a typical way engagements are structured. We adjust the detail, pace and level of support to match your capacity and objectives.

1. Initial discussion

We discuss your current situation, goals and challenges to confirm which services will add the most value.

2. Proposal & scope

You receive a clear, written proposal outlining scope, deliverables, timelines and responsibilities.

3. Delivery & engagement

We deliver agreed services through a combination of on-site work, remote sessions and documentation, as appropriate.

4. Handover & follow-up

We ensure key people are equipped to continue, share recommendations for next steps and agree on any ongoing support needed.

Organisations we support

Our services are suitable for a range of organisations – from smaller businesses that are formalising their SHEQ practices for the first time to more established operations seeking independent support.

  • Industrial and manufacturing operations.
  • Warehousing, logistics and distribution.
  • Construction and project-based environments.
  • Service providers and office-based environments.
  • Public sector and community-focused organisations.
