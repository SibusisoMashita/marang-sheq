SHEQ Management Systems

Structured systems for consistent, reliable SHEQ performance.

A well-designed management system brings together your people, processes and tools so that Safety, Health, Environmental and Quality requirements are planned, implemented and improved in a consistent way.

Marang SHEQ Consulting works with you to develop and implement integrated SHEQ management systems that fit your operations, support legal compliance and create a strong foundation for ongoing improvement.

What is a SHEQ management system?

A SHEQ management system is the framework your organisation uses to manage risks, meet legal obligations and continually improve performance in Safety, Health, Environment and Quality. It defines how responsibilities, processes, documents and records work together in a structured way.

Depending on your context, your system may be aligned with recognised standards and guidelines and integrated with other business systems such as operations, HR and maintenance.

We help you design a system that is appropriate for your size, complexity and risk profile – avoiding unnecessary bureaucracy while ensuring robust control.

Typical system elements
  • SHEQ policy and objectives.
  • Roles, responsibilities and organisational structure.
  • Risk assessment and legal compliance processes.
  • Operational controls, procedures and permits.
  • Training, communication and awareness mechanisms.
  • Incident, nonconformity and corrective action processes.
  • Monitoring, auditing and management review.
  • Documentation and records management.

Our approach to system development

We follow a structured, collaborative approach that reflects the Plan–Do–Check–Act (PDCA) cycle used in many management system standards.

01 · Plan
Understand & design

We review your context, risks, legal requirements and existing controls, then design a system framework that is practical for your organisation.

02 · Do
Develop & implement

We help you develop procedures, tools and documentation, and support implementation through training, coaching and on-site assistance.

03 · Check
Monitor & measure

Together we put in place monitoring, inspections, internal audits and other checks to track performance and identify gaps.

04 · Act
Improve & sustain

We support you with corrective actions, management reviews and improvement initiatives to keep the system effective over time.

Benefits of a structured SHEQ management system

Improved compliance

Clear processes, responsibilities and records to demonstrate compliance with SHEQ legislation and requirements.

Risk reduction

Systematic identification and control of risks, reducing the likelihood and impact of incidents and nonconformities.

Operational consistency

Standardised ways of working that support reliable outcomes and make it easier to train and integrate new team members.

Better decisions

Improved data, reporting and review processes to support informed decision-making and prioritisation.

Engaged people

Clear expectations, communication and participation that help build a positive safety, health and quality culture.

Business value

Reduced disruptions and losses, enhanced reputation and improved ability to win and retain business where SHEQ performance is critical.
